About

My Medicine Cabinet was created to help keep you informed about current medication recalls and adverse events. It is also simple to use.

  1. Start by creating your profile. Either fill the form out on the home page or visit Create Profile.
  2. Next add medications. Go to the My Medications page. In the search box start typing the name your medication. Once you see your medication in the list, select it and click Add. Your medication will appear under Current Medications.
  3. View Details. Under Current Medications you will see a list of medications you have added. Click the View Details button.
  4. Keep your list up to date. To remove a medication from your list, click the View Details and the Remove button.
